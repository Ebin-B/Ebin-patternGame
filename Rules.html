Rules to play

  • First user should click on the screen
  • Then the heading changes to level one. That time one button will blink you have to click on that button
  • The level changes to two that time next level 2 button will blink and you have to click both level1 and level 2 button
  • If you click correct button then it changes to level 3 and blink another one. Then you have to click all level 1,2and 3 level button
  • This pattern will continue until you click wrong one

NOTE:Same button will be repeated in different level
